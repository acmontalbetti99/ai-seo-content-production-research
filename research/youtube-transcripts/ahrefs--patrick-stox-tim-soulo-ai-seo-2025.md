# Research Notes: Ahrefs Data Studies on AI Content & SEO
## Authors: Patrick Stox, Tim Soulo, Ryan Law — Ahrefs

## Study 1: 900,000 New Pages (May 2025)

- 74.2% of newly created web pages contain AI-generated content
- 2.5% "pure AI", 71.7% "AI-assisted", 25.8% "pure human"

> Tim Soulo: "What if the 25.8% of human-only content is what actually cuts through the noise? Simply because it feels different to all the regurgitated AI slop?"

## Study 2: AI Search Visitors Convert 23x Higher (June 2025)

- AI search traffic = 0.5% of total visits but generated 12.1% of all signups
- Conversion rate: AI search visitors convert 23x higher than traditional organic
- AI traffic grew ~9.7x over previous 12 months

## Patrick Stox at Ahrefs Evolve 2025: "GEO? AEO? LLMO?"

- AI search does not replace traditional search; it extends it

 > Ry an Law: "Google still sends far more traffic. And it's still your best marketing channel."

> Tim Soulo: "YouTube is the second largest search engine. They're in total driving less than 1% of your traffic. YouTube might be a lot more. If you're not doing it, go do more video right now."

## Key Takeaways

1. 71.7% of new content is AI-assisted - new baseline
2. AI search visitors convert 23x higher - getting cited by ChatGPT is valuable
3. YouTube is bigger opportunity than AEO right now
4. Create citable content - data studies, original research
