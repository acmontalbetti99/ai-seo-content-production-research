## Expert Selection Criteria

These 10 experts were selected based on:
- **Active practitioners**, not just commentators — they run agencies, tools, or content operations
- **Consistent public output** in 2024–2026 (YouTube, LinkedIn, or newsletter)
- **Distinct angle** on AI + SEO (not all saying the same thing)
- **Verifiable results** — real clients, real traffic data, or software they've built and sell
