# Other Research Materials

## 1. Kevin Indig — Growth Memo Newsletter (Selected Issues, 2024–2025)

**Source:** https://www.kevin-indig.com/growth-memo/  
**Collected:** April 2026

Former VP SEO at Shopify (world's largest e-commerce platform). Now advises high-growth SaaS companies including Ramp, Bounce, Hims, Nextdoor, Snapchat.

### AI Content is high-ROI for:
- FAQ and support documentation
- Product spec pages
- Programmatic location/comparison pages

### AI Content is low-ROI when:
- Thought leadership and opinion pieces
- Original research and proprietary data
- Case studies and client stories

> "The bottleneck in most content programs isn't writing speed — it's strategic clarity about what to write. AI solves the wrong problem for most companies."

## 2. Eli Schwartz — Product-Led SEO

**Book:** *Product-Led SEO* (2021)

### Content that gets eaten by AI Overviews:
- "What is X" informational articles
- "How to do Y" generic guides

### Content that survives:
- Proprietary data
- First-person experience
- Product-led content
- Competitor comparisons with genuine differentiation
